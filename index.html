    Jiemin Fang (方傑民)¶

    Email:
    GitHub | Google Scholar | DBLP

    I am a final-year PhD candidate at the Institute of Artificial Intelligence and School of Electronic Information and Communications (EIC), Huazhong University of Science and Technology (HUST), supervised by Prof. Wenyu Liu and Prof. Xinggang Wang. I received my B.E. degree from EIC, HUST in 2018. My research interests mainly lie in efficient deep learning from neural rendering and neural architecture perspectives.

    If you are interested in intern or full-time positions at Huawei Cloud, welcome to contact me via email.
