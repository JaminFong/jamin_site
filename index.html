    Jiemin Fang (方傑民)¶

    I am a senior researcher and assistant chief expert at Huawei of the TopMinds program, leading a small group that mainly works on 3D generation and neural rendering. My research interests also include representation learning, neural architecture design/search and AutoML.

    I obtained my PhD and BE degree from the School of Electronic Information and Communications (EIC) at Huazhong University of Science and Technology (HUST) in 2023 and 2018 respectively, supervised by Prof. Wenyu Liu and Prof. Xinggang Wang.
